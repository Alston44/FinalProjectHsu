Esuer is an online second-hand clothing shop designed for adolescents to middle-aged adults of all genders. The platform makes browsing, buying, and selling pre-loved clothes simple and accessible, promoting confidence in purchasing second-hand items. It encourages users to contribute by selling their own clothing and offers fresh outfit ideas and styling inspiration. With a user-friendly, intuitive design, Esuer ensures easy navigation without any learning curve.

A translator doesn’t just interpret barks! It gives helpful tips based on what your dog is feeling. If your dog sounds anxious, it might suggest a quieter spot or a favorite toy. It also tracks bark patterns to build a personalized model, predicting behavior based on time, context, and past actions. Over time, it learns your dog’s unique bark style and emotions. You can even save or play barks for fun.

Clarity is a productivity app designed to help users live intentionally by aligning their tasks with effective time management. It integrates task timers, calendar syncing, and customizable categories to promote mindful planning and organization. With a clean, minimal interface, Clarity focuses on not just what users do but why, fostering self-awareness and productivity. Future plans include AI-driven insights, cross-platform syncing, and community building around intentional living.
